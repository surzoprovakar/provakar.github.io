CS5594 - Blockchain Technologies (Spring 2021)

Course Description

The course will explore the principles of emerging blockchain technologies. The course will first cover basic data structures and cryptographic building blocks in blockchain such as distributed systems, cryptographic primitives including cryptographic hash functions, asymmetric cryptography, digital signatures. The course will then focus on critical blockchain components and infrastructures such as distributed ledger, consensus protocols, cryptocurrencies and smart contracts. The course will also cover several recent advancements in blockchains such as privacy-preserving blockchain, decentralized applications. The successful students are expected to obtain a good understanding of cryptographic primitives and blockchain essentials, which will be a unique skill for competitive R&D positions in industry.
